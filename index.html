Hyderabad Parks For People is open source and citizen-maintained. If you spot an outdated official, a missed court order, a broken link, or have a verified update, please write in. We respond within 48 hours and credit contributors (or stay anonymous on request).

  • Auto-fetched news items are labeled "pending verification" and never enter the main timeline without human review.
